Why Build This
What traditional call analytics can't tell you

Gong gives you the talk ratio. Chorus flags the keyword. Neither tells you why the deal stalled — which objection ended the eval, what feature gap cost you the win, or which message lands with a VP Eng versus a CISO. By the time any of that surfaces, the moment has passed.

Traditional Analytics
  • Talk ratio: 72% rep / 28% prospect
  • Keywords flagged: pricing, timeline, budget
  • Stage updated: Discovery → Qualifying
  • Delivered: next-day dashboard
This Approach
  • AE skipped economic impact — SPICED discovery gap
  • No EB access in 3 consecutive calls — deal at risk
  • Vercel pricing fear is your strongest entry wedge
  • Delivered: minutes after the call, per stakeholder
